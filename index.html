A little bit about

Behnam (Ben) Nikkhah

Thanks for taking a look at my work! I'm just a simple guy looking to develop his digital literacy through web development. I constantly take courses to improve myself in both front and back-end development. I honestly believe that the biggest challenge in web development is the design of the website. While a website needs to portray information, it also needs to portray it in a fun and interactive way to keep viewers interested and engaged.

One type of design that I truly enjoy is the parallax. Most websites display a basic, one-layer parallax consisting of a fixed background attachment and some text to over it. However, I wanted to take that to a new perspective on my profile page. That's why here, on the top of my page, you can view this multi-layer parallax design. In fact, I got my inspiration from the Firewatch Game website. The multi-layer parallax gives much more depth and creates a sense of realism to the design.

Do not seek praise. Seek criticism.
